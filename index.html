Education

Education empowers children to have a secure future and grow up as responsible citizens. The Right to Education (RTE) Act made education free and compulsory for children aged 6-14 years.

Healthcare

The geospatial diversity of the country exacerbates issues of healthcare accessibility and availability. With 65% of the population in rural areas and urban slum dwellers prioritizing survival over healthcare, the challenges are significant.

Women Empowerment

Gender equality is crucial for the health and development of nations. Promoting equal rights and access to resources for women is key to sustainable development goals.
